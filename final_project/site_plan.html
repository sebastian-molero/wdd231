Site Plan - A New Companion

1. Site Name

Name: A New Companion

Reason: It supports the goal of connecting people with pets who need a new home

2. Site Purpose

The site connects people with pets available for adoption, also providing tips to have a better experience both adopting process and taking care of the animals

3. Scenarios

  • How can I adopt a pet through this site?
  • What steps should I take to prepare my home for a new pet?
  • Where can I find tips on caring for newly adopted animals?
  • What guarantees foes the site offer about the pets' health?

4. Color Scheme

  • Primary color: #2e7d32
  • Secondary color: #e8f5e9
  • Accent color: #fdd835bd

5. Typography

  • Headings: Quicksand, sans-serif
  • Body Text: Open Sans, serif
